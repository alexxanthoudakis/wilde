Wilde Trials International News Archive

April 1st 1895 — June 15th, 1895

Oscar Wilde Arrested!

The Wilde Trials International News Archive gathers news reports covering the three criminal trials of anglo-irish playwright Oscar Wilde. Wilde’s three trials were a global media event in 1895 and were front page news the world over for nearly a two month period, triggering debates about homosexuality, family relations, censorship and press standards, literary Aestheticism, and British criminal jurisprudence. Readers can now browse, search, and compare news reports from around the world and track how they moved and were transformed across borders and languages.
